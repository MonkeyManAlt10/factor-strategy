• Top-10 adds +6.8 pp CAGR over top-50, but at the cost of +7.2 pp more vol
  and an extra -11.1 pp of max drawdown.
• Top-50 Sharpe (1.15) beats top-10 Sharpe (1.09) — top-50 is more efficient

  concentration in the market's current momentum leaders (e.g. tech in bull runs).
• Information Ratio: top-10 (0.63) is meaningfully higher than top-50 (0.36),
  suggesting the concentrated bets are adding active return efficiently — but
  this is entirely in-sample.
